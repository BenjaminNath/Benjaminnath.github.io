About Me

hello.

I'm Ben and I'm a web developer from Pittsburgh, PA.

Being born and raised in Pittsburgh, I'm a front-end web developer with an appreciation for minimalist design who is looking to start a career in the city I love.

I graduated from the University of Dayton in May, where I studied Computer Information Systems. Since then, I have been focusing on front-end web development for desktop and mobile. I love to learn new technologies and face new challenges. I spend most of my days searching for inspiration in different designs, experimenting with different frameworks (Bootstrap, React.js, Angular.js), or reading articles about the latest techngology.

Outside of coding, some of my other hobbies include traveling, skateboarding, reading, and of course, drinking coffee. Everything in life is worth trying at least once.
